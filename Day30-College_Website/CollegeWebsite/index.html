PRINCIPAL


It is a great pleasure to welcome students at SNDT College of Home Science, a Constituent College of SNDT Women’s University, Mumbai founded by Maharshi Karve.


A 56 years old College offering both English & Marathi medium.

The College offers all the five specializations of Home Science.

I am honoured to provide you the best quality education. We at SNDT College of Home Science offer courses which build your career in the future.


My mission is goal oriented, help you to develop a multi- faceted personality and strive to excel in curricular and extracurricular activities.

I welcome you all to our College, a great institution of higher learning which will help you to bloom into empowered Home Scientists and sensitive human beings.


From,


Dr. Vrushali Nagarale
